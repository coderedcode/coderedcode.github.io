Dragon Innovation

Dragon Innovation makes the supremely hard task of manufacturing consumer electronics at scale feel easy and empowers innovators to focus on and develop their core idea.

Code Red Code assisted in the development of a searchable database of contract manufacturers worldwide to speed up the process of entrepreneurs' identifying and vetting potential partners.

Dragon Innovation was acquired by Avnet in the summer of 2017.
