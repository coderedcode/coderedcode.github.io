TurningArt

TurningArt offers turnkey artwork services to commercial and residential property owners, managers, and tenants across the United States.

From the first post-seed funding round in 2012 until the summer of 2020, I led the development team and designed, written, and deployed code for all public marketing pages and internal tools for sales, operations, finance, and artwork management.

What began with a series of pivots from a consumer-focused e-commerce site is now a profitable B2B company whose product is used by organizations ranging from Fortune 500 companies like CBRE to universities like Harvard and startups like EnerNOC.
